About Customer Merge
Match Confidence
  • Strong - High confidence duplicates (same address, very similar names + state)
  • Weak - Lower confidence (similar names, same email domain)
Merge Process
  • Select which customer to keep as "Master"
  • All distributions, notes, and sales orders from the duplicate are moved to the master
  • Non-null fields from duplicate are merged into master if master field is empty
  • The duplicate customer is deleted
  • All actions are recorded in the audit trail
